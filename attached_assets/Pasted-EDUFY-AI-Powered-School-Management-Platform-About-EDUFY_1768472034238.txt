EDUFY - AI-Powered School Management Platform
About EDUFY
EDUFY is an enterprise-grade, multi-tenant AI-powered school management SaaS platform that provides a comprehensive three-way communication system connecting students (and parents), teachers, and administrators. The platform delivers intelligent insights into academics, attendance, and performance through role-specific interfaces.

Core Features
For Students & Parents:

View class timetables and exam schedules
Track attendance records with detailed history
Access published report cards and grades
View homework assignments with due dates
Receive real-time notifications for attendance, marks, announcements
Message teachers directly
AI-powered performance insights and risk indicators
Fee payment tracking and history
School announcements and news feed
For Teachers:

Mark and manage student attendance
Upload marks with duplicate detection and absent tracking
Create and assign homework with deadlines
View class leaderboards with performance analytics
Manage class and exam timetables (class teachers)
Send announcements to students/parents
Direct messaging with students and parents
AI insights for at-risk students identification
Report card workflow with publish/review system
Request class access (subject teachers)
For Administrators:

Complete user management (bulk import via Excel)
Manage classes, sections, and subjects
Fee management and configuration
School-wide exam and timetable scheduling
Access request approval workflow
Comprehensive reporting with Excel export
AI-powered school analytics dashboard
Audit history and revision tracking
Multi-tenant school configuration
Real-time notifications and announcements

Website Design Recommendations
Suggested Color Scheme for Website:

Hero/Primary CTA: Indigo #6366F1 - Conveys trust, education, technology
Secondary CTA: Emerald #10B981 - Fresh, growth, success
Accent Highlights: Amber #F59E0B - Energy, attention, warmth
Background: Clean whites #FAFAFA with soft indigo tints #F0F0FF
Dark Mode: Deep navy #0D0D14 with purple undertones
Design Style:

Modern, clean, and professional aesthetic
Rounded corners (12-20px border radius)
Subtle shadows for depth and card elevation
Smooth spring-based animations
Glass-morphism effects for premium feel
Role-based color coding (Student=Indigo, Teacher=Green, Admin=Amber)
Typography:

System fonts for performance (SF Pro, Segoe UI, Roboto)
Bold headings (600-700 weight)
Regular body text (400 weight)
Clear hierarchy: H1 (28px), H2 (22px), H3 (18px), Body (16px), Caption (12px)
Key Visual Elements:

AI-themed graphics (neural networks, insights, analytics)
